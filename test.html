    You've started your business, now learn how to operate it

    Recent studies show 20% percent of small businesses fail by the first year, 30% by the second, 50% by the fifth and by the 10th year, a staggering 70% of businesses have shut off their lights. For minorities, the numbers can be even more daunting.

    Eight out of 10 Black-owned businesses fail within the first 18 months. 

    Operations School is on a mission to change that.

    About Us 

    Since 2019, Operations School has been providing operations based education and consulting services. Our mission is to train, empower and equip startup and nonprofit leaders with the skills to successfully operate and scale their operations. 

    We envision a world where minority business owners are no longer disadvantaged, they receive the skills, training and resources needed to thrive and are operational within 10 years of launching. 

    It's Time to Get Your Business Legit

    GET YOUR BUSINESS LEGIT is our signature 9 week program that provides entrepreneurs with our proprietary business operations roadmap. By the end of this course, you'll have the tools necessary to operate a successful business capable of being competitive in today's market.
